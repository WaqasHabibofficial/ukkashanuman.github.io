The Studio

Wade Design Architects is a nationally recognized, full-service architecture firm based in Northern California focused on private residences, specialty retail concepts, wineries and hospitality projects. Wade Design was founded by principals Ani and Luke Wade in 2007, after working as lead designers and project managers at Backen Gillam Architects.

We practice a collaborative art, which begins by listening intently to our client’s goals and desires. From initial concepts through to construction completion, we strive to transform their aspirations into reality. We believe a fulfilling process for everyone involved ultimately leads to the best possible result and the most rewarding experience.

The firm’s award-winning work has been celebrated in numerous design publications, including Architectural Digest, Food & Wine, California Home + Design, Marin Magazine, Dwell, Sonoma Magazine, Luxe Interiors + Design, Bloomberg, Dering Hall, California Homes and the San Francisco Chronicle, among others.

Luke Wade

Principal

A native Texan, Luke began practicing in the San Francisco Bay Area after receiving his Bachelor of Architecture degree from the University of Texas at Austin. Working closely with Howard Backen, he honed his design skills as a project manager with Backen Gillam Architects and oversaw projects ranging from private residences to wineries, retail concepts, restaurants and resorts. In 2007 Luke co-founded Wade Design Architects with his wife and partner Ani Wade. 

In addition to projects in Northern California, work has taken Luke across the nation, and to beautiful sites in the wine growing regions of Oregon, Washington and Canada. He has consulted for notable clients Williams-Sonoma Inc. and The Sundance Group. Beyond North America, he has designed projects in Australia, Dubai, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait and Panama.

On weekends you can find Luke outdoors with his family, enjoying the trails and beaches of Marin County. His desire to travel and see the world is mostly insatiable, there is always more to learn.

Ani Wade

Principal

Ani grew up in Peru and spent her formative years in Costa Rica, then began her architectural training in the United States. After receiving her Bachelor of Architecture degree from UT Austin, she excelled as a designer and project manager at Bay Area firms. At Backen Gillam Architects, her skills were focused on highly crafted residential projects and the design and construction of the Solage Resort in Calistoga. In 2007 Ani co-founded Wade Design Architects with her husband and partner Luke Wade. Ani brings a commitment to clarity and organization to every project.  She enjoys all aspects of the architecture practice and will find the time to take on senior project management roles while at the same time ensuring the business side of the practice is running smoothly. She relies on the help of many at Wade Design, to juggle her wide-ranging responsibilities.

In her spare time, Ani finds an escape by building her yoga practice which helps her maintain balance while juggling being a mother and a professional.

Jenny Hoover

Sr Project Manager

Jenny lived in Southern California and Washington before graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture from Rice University in Houston in 2001.

After a brief stint with Gensler in Santa Monica, she settled in the San Francisco Bay Area where in 2007, she met Luke and Ani Wade through friends in the Bay Area network of architects.

Jenny is inspired by every sort of architecture, from humble or makeshift structures that serve their function with efficiency, to extreme or capricious forms that serve little more purpose than to distort the senses. She enjoys working to find a balance between those two extremes.

Nicholas Koelzer

Project Manager

Nick received his Bachelor of Architecture degree from the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, just a stone’s throw from his hometown.

He grew up in a family that followed the ‘do-it-yourself’ mantra, so Nick jumped into many carpentry projects at an early age. It was this exposure to small-scale building that led him to appreciate the more intimate aspects of design that make each project unique.

Outside of the firm, he enjoys crafting furniture, playing roller hockey, and inventing toys for his cat, Stevie. Nick is a licensed California architect and has been on the Wade Design team for 7 years.
