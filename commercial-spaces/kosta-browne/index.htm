Site by

Kosta Browne

Kosta Browne’s inviting tasting room, “The Gallery”, is set inside Sebastopol’s Barlow space, with design cues prioritized around showcasing the winemaking process. A communal mezzanine space overlooks the winery’s fermentation room, creating a sensory lounge and bar that connects to this exclusive behind-the-scenes working area. A rich and moody aesthetic weaves craftsmanship in design with classic, mid-century saloon vibes, welcoming guests to an elevated tasting experience.
