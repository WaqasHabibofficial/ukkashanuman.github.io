Harmony Meadows

This relaxed, luxurious getaway was dreamed to serve as a tranquil getaway where tennis and entertainment take center stage.  Tucked in between the orchards and mountains off the shores of Lake Chelan, Washington, Harmony Meadows’ crisp, white barn style structures offer a welcoming home for special celebrations and weekend getaways, where expanses of space are connected both indoor and out. Phase 2 of design conception is currently in progress for this jewel site.
