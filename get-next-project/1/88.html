Takeda

DUBAI, UAE 2020 2136 SQ M

The brief

To design an office that rivalled their new head office in Tokyo, Japan

Our approach

Celebrate the company’s Japanese heritage while giving a nod to the local context.

The concept curates Japanese design elements in a minimal, restrained, architectural language with shoji screen geometry and earthy materiality.

Takeda is based on four values: Integrity, Fairness, Honesty and Perseverance. These values are evident in the material selection and the fact that this office achieved a LEED silver rating.
