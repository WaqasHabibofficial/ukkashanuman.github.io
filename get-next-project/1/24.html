McKinsey

Doha, Qatar 2020 720 SQ M

The brief

To create a future-proofed office, not just for the McKinsey workforce of today, but for the workforce of the next decade.

Our approach

The design is anchored on three pillars: the culture of the country, health & wellness, and Digital McKinsey and throughout the design, we were very conscious that these should be baked into the cake, rather than just decorative add-ons.

Design and technology worked hand-in-glove on this one.

The way we interpreted McKinsey Digital was embedded in the way we work, and the way we collaborate.
