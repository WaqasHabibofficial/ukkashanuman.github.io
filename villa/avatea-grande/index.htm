Magnificent, majestic and one-of-a-kind, Villa Avatea stands alone as the only one of her type.

Envisioned in the Grande architectural style, Villa Avatea embodies lavish living at its island best, from the marina-worthy basement boat garage to the myriad pools (including saltwater, vitality and lap), a lazy river, sunken sun beds, open-air atriums across three floors, beach bar, retractable platforms and more.

Villa Avatea is designed for those who truly believe that life is meant to be lived, surrounded by their loved ones in sumptuous environments.
