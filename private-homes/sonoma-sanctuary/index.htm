Sonoma Sanctuary

Perched on a picturesque Sonoma Mountain overlook, this weekend retreat was designed as a family space to gather, relax and enjoy the tranquil wine country landscape from every room. Clean lines and an open layout allow for relaxed living and entertaining. Its front welcomes guests with elements of East Coast architecture, while the rear of the residence ties in a California wine country aesthetic, bridging the comfortable interior with its natural surroundings.
