Sonoma Mountain Hideaway

Situated among ancient oaks and olive trees, this Sonoma Mountain residence was envisioned as both a vacation getaway and a future retirement haven, designed with elegant comfort at its core. The property features a Main House, an ADU, and an expansive outdoor entertaining area, together forming an idyllic retreat for a couple who long dreamed of settling in wine country. A neutral element palette of natural wood, stone and glass harmonize seamlessly with the surrounding landscape. Inside, the heart of the home is a breathtaking great room, anchored by a cedar plank-clad truss system that evokes the presence of a central ridge beam. This distinctive architectural feature intersects with a row of clerestory windows, flooding the space with natural light. A double-sided stone fireplace, expansive sliding glass doors, and an open-concept kitchen contribute to the home's clean lines and communal ambiance. Outdoors, dining, lounge areas and a spa provide the perfect setting to relax, unwind, and take in the stunning terrain. The home is at once humbly understated and quietly awe-inspiring -  a true hideaway in the heart of Sonoma.
