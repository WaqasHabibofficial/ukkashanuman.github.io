Sonoma Craftsman

This charming Craftsman bungalow is set on a long, rectangular property in the heart of Sonoma’s historic district and just blocks from its main square. The design challenge was to modernize the classic home while connecting it to an additional, new space. A series of courtyards was created, linking the front of the home to the back of the property. This careful assemblage of spaces gives the clients endless connection to the outdoors.
