The Light of West Marin

This idyllic home, set among West Marin’s wild land, interacts with nature at every turn. The team took inspiration from San Geronimo’s untamed landscape, the property’s western sun exposure, and cooling marine breezes. A boomerang-shaped, low-lying compound was designed where light would permeate rooms from every angle, and the house itself would lead guests outside through a series of breezeways and sliding doors. This residence is truly a home inspired by and connected to nature.
