Napa Cabin

A cozy “jewel-box” cabin nestled among surrounding vineyards and forest, utilizes unaltered natural light and materiality to replicate the feeling of a walk under the native redwoods. The small scale allowed for attention paid to every detail, reinforcing the design thesis for tailored solutions. Soaring interior ceilings and a serene wraparound porch expand the space and blur the line between inside and out.
