Site by

In Town - St. Helena

Wade Design was commissioned to create a dream retirement home on the last undeveloped lot in St. Helena’s historic westside. The uniquely shaped 10,000 sq ft lot offered both opportunity and complexity. Anchored by a diagonal layout, the main house, guest quarters, pool, and outdoor spaces are thoughtfully connected through courtyards and breezeways to maximize space, light, and flow. The architecture is a study in balance. Traditional shingle and modern metal meet across the roofline. Windows of mixed styles echo the home's layered character. The home's entry sets the tone: formal yet relaxed, timeless and inviting. Designed to house the clients’ grand piano, the foyer opens into a thoughtfully composed living space featuring an arched wine cellar, dramatic two-story fireplace, and at its center, a light-filled kitchen. Outside, a private pool and wraparound porch create seamless indoor-outdoor living, perfect for quiet evenings or hosting neighbors. The residence celebrates the energy of its vibrant neighborhood while giving the owners a peaceful space to relax, recharge, and entertain with ease.
