Weekends in Sonoma

Set on 3 acres in the heart of Sonoma wine country, this contemporary yet cozy compound was created for weekend getaways and entertaining. The estate includes a main residence, pool house and covered outdoor entertaining space equipped with a kitchen and pizza oven.  The large, rectangular pool serves as the strong axis point to all.  A few of its many unique attributes include the front façade, which features a cantilevered upper story clad in wood with gabled roof meant to perch over the ground level, with its cleaner contemporary geometries.  The statement entry space is additionally memorable with its glass and steel railing and transparent wall to usher in views of Mt. Veeder and beyond.
