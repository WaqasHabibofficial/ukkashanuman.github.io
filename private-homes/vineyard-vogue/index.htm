Vineyard Vogue

Rooted in the charm of wine country's rural farmsteads, this residence reimagines the classic “farm cluster” layout with a modern, quietly luxurious sensibility. Designed for a couple who balance creativity and connection, the St. Helena residence unfolds through a series of indoor-outdoor spaces that move effortlessly between solitude and celebration. A board-formed concrete fireplace and glass-encased wine lounge anchor the interiors, where natural textures and moody tones play against vineyard views. A party barn strikes the perfect balance of sophistication and playfulness. It’s a space designed for friends, family, laughter, and late nights. The result is a home that feels grounded yet glamorous - an elegant fusion of wine-country calm and modern edge.
